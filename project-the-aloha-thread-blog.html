Helping to develop a website has shown me how important it is to understand and implement UI/UX design. I also learned about API's and their importance in connecting different applications together and feeding real time information onto the page.

Check out the website: The Aloha Thread

UI/UX designs

By combining clean typography, high-quality images, and a responsive layout, we created a website that is both beautiful and functional.

UI Design
  • The website features a clean and minimalist design, characterized by a predominantly white and beige color scheme that exudes a sense of aesthetic sophistication.
  • The typography is clear and readable, with headings and subheadings used to create a clear hierarchy of information.
  • The website features high-quality images and graphics, to emphasize the overall beauty of the Hawaiian culture and lifestyle.
  • The images incorporated into the design thoughtfully convey cultural authenticity, adding depth and nuance to the overall aesthetic.
UX Design
  • User journey is carefully crafted to create a seamless experience.
  • The website's navigation is creates a seamless experience that is simple and intuitive, with a clear menu bar at the top.
  • Content is organized into clear categories, making it easy for users to find what they're looking for
  • The website's layout is responsive, meaning it adapts to different screen sizes and devices, ensuring a smooth user experience across various platforms.
  • The website's loading speed is fast, which is essential for providing a good user experience.
