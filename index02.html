ALOHA!

The Aloha Thread is a resource, information, and shopping page for those interested in gaining an understanding of Hawaiian culture and exploring sewing Asian/ Polynesian infused outfits.

Every week, a new Quote or Proverb is posted under Hawaiian Quotes and Proberbs The topics would include the history and cultivation of native hawaiian plants, inspirations for timeless designs, architecture inspirations and its connection to clothing and lifestyle, living sustainably, and the hawaiian poem/ quote of the week.
