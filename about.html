About us

Welcome to The Aloha Thread!

The Aloha Thread is a resource and inspiration site for those interested in gaining an understanding of Hawaiian culture and sewing Asian/ Polynesian infused outfits. Many of our inspired designs incoporate a variety of lifestyles such as surfing, hiking, gardening, boating, beaching, and working in the field or garden. These designs also infuse cultural clothing from the East coast of Asia to with Western clothing.

We hope to inspire you to create your own personal clothing!
