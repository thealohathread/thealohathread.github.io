Aloha!

My name is Carissa O'Connell and I am the owner and designer of The Aloha Thread.

The Aloha Thread is a place where you will find unique and inspirational clothing that has been infused with culture and a variety of lifestyles. Each garment is a handmade design and meant to be worn in layers.
